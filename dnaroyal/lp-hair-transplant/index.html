Hair Transplant Process

DNA Royal offers advanced, painless hair transplant with expert care, natural results, minimal downtime, and long-lasting confidence.

Consultation The first crucial step is a comprehensive consultation with a qualified hair restoration expert. The counselor, with the help of a hair transplant surgeon, will assess your scalp to determine the grade of baldness, the extent of hair loss and the potential donor area.
Hairline Design Based on the evaluation and your desired outcome, the surgeon will create a personalized treatment plan that goes beyond just the number of grafts. This plan incorporates factors like your facial features, existing hair pattern, scalp laxity & desired hairline style.
Extraction Direct Hair Transplant (DHT) utilizes a specialized method that minimizes trauma to the scalp. This ensures healthy grafts with a higher survival rate, ultimately leading to a denser, more natural-looking head of hair.
Implanting Grafts Extracted grafts bypass storage solutions, which can potentially compromise their viability. DHT prioritizes immediate implantation into the recipient area, maximizing the number of healthy follicles that thrive in their new location.
After-care Following the procedure, the scalp may experience some swelling and tenderness, which typically subsides within a few days. The Post-surgery care team will provide detailed post-operative instructions to promote healing and optimize graft survival.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a hair transplant permanent?

Yes, the results of a hair transplant are typically permanent. The transplanted hair follicles are taken from donor areas (usually the back or sides of the scalp), which are genetically resistant to hair loss, ensuring long-lasting growth.

Is the hair transplant procedure painful?

Not at all. We perform the procedure under local anesthesia, ensuring a virtually pain-free experience. Patients remain awake, relaxed, and comfortable throughout the treatment.

How long does the hair transplant take?

The duration depends on the number of grafts required, but most procedures take 48 hours and are completed in a single day.

When will I see the results?

New hair typically starts growing around 34 months after the procedure. Full results are visible within 912 months, including thickness, density, and natural growth pattern.

Will the transplanted hair look natural?

Absolutely. At DNA Royal, we specialize in natural hairline design and precision graft placement to ensure the results match your facial aesthetics perfectly.

Can women undergo a hair transplant too?

Yes, hair transplants are equally effective for women experiencing pattern thinning, hormonal hair loss, or scalp trauma. Our procedures are tailored to suit female hair patterns and density.

Are there any side effects or risks?

Hair transplant is a safe and low-risk procedure when performed by trained professionals. Mild swelling or redness may occur temporarily, but major side effects are rare under expert care.

What is the recovery time after the procedure?

You can return to normal activities in 23 days. Full recovery of the scalp usually takes 710 days, and we provide complete post-procedure care instructions.

How do I know if Im a good candidate for a hair transplant?

If you have noticeable thinning or bald patches, a stable donor area, and are in overall good health, you're likely a suitable candidate. We conduct a detailed scalp analysis during your consultation to confirm eligibility.

What is the cost of a hair transplant at DNA Royal?

Costs vary depending on the number of grafts and the technique used (FUE, DHI, etc.). We offer affordable pricing, EMI options, and complimentary consultations to help you plan your journey with ease.
