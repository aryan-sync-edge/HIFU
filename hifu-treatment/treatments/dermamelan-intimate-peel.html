What causes intimate hyperpigmentation?

Intimate hyperpigmentation occurs when there is excessive melanin production that darkens the skin within the genital-perianal area, inner thighs and groin. 

  • Age - biological, biochemical & molecular mechanisms which cause aging of the vaginal tissue can result in pigmentation.
  • Genetics Predisposition - more common with high phototype individuals (IV, V & VI). 
  • Hormonal Changes - an increase in oestrogen levels stimulates activity of the Tyrosinase enzyme. 
  • Care & Beauty Habits - hair removal methods can lead to abrasion & folliculitis, which triggers skin inflammation that can lead to increased melanin production. 
  • Clothing Habits - tight clothing creates friction on the skin which causes the skin to naturally thicken and become darker. This often affects the inner thighs with use of tight pants or underwear. 

What is the Dermamelan Intimate Peel?

Dermamelan Intimate Peel is a non-invasive treatment by Mesoestetic that offers a unique and effective way of treating pigmentation in just one session, helping to improve the quality and appearance of the intimate area. In addition to its depigmentation benefits, its antiaging action enhances skin elasticity, making it an all-in-one treatment. 

The products that are applied incorporate innovative active ingredients which brighten and rejuvenate the skin. Treatment is carried out by our discreet and professional expert medical team, ensuring your comfort and privacy at all times.

How does the Dermamelan Intimate Peel work?

Stage 1 - Intensive depigmentation carried out at the clinic 

The peel features a unique and potent combination of ingredients which increase cell renewal and treat the root cause of pigmentation. Developed exclusively by Mesoestetic, the [meso] Intimate System ensures maximum efficacy by integrating active ingredients that work alongside an innovative encapsulation system. This allows for controlled action, high skin tolerability, moisturising and repairing, and long-term maintenance of active ingredient effects. [GL] Inflamm, also exclusive to Mesoestetic, helps control inflammation, enhance progressive depigmentation, and support the skin’s defence mechanisms for reduced hypersensitivity reactions and restored skin balance. 

Once applied to the intimate area, the peel is left on for 3-4 hours. After removal, a hydrating and soothing mask is applied over the treated area and left on for 10-15 minutes. 

Stage 2 - At-home use treatment

Results are maintained with an at-home, daily use Depigmenting Gel Cream. Using ingredients such as Kojic Acid, Phytic Acid and Salicylic Acid, It treats and regulates genital hyperpigmentation, enhances elasticity, protects and moisturises, and improves skin barrier function of the vulval epithelium. Clinical results have shown a 60% reduction in pigmentation and 27% decrease in transepidermal water loss (TEWL).
